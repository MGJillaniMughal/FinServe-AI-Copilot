KNOW YOUR CUSTOMER (KYC) POLICY

1. Identity Verification
Customers must provide government-issued identification verified at onboarding.

2. Customer Due Diligence
Risk-based due diligence is performed. High-risk customers are re-verified annually.

3. Data Protection (GDPR)
Personal data is processed lawfully. Data subjects may request access and erasure.
Consent is recorded and data-retention schedules are documented.
