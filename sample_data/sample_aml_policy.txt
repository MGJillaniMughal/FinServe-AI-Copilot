ANTI-MONEY LAUNDERING (AML) POLICY

1. Purpose
This Anti-Money Laundering policy establishes controls to detect and prevent money
laundering and terrorist financing in line with FATF recommendations.

2. Customer Due Diligence (KYC)
All customers undergo Know Your Customer identity verification at onboarding.
Enhanced due diligence applies to politically exposed persons and high-risk customers.

3. Transaction Monitoring
Suspicious activity is monitored continuously. Thresholds trigger alerts and review.
Confirmed suspicious activity results in a Suspicious Activity Report (SAR) filing.

4. Record Keeping
Records are retained for the period required by applicable regulation.
