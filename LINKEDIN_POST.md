# FinServe AI Copilot - LinkedIn Post

A fintech client came to us with a real problem.

Their risk and compliance team was drowning in documents. Financial reports, AML and KYC policies, audit files, investment research. Every review was manual, slow, and inconsistent. They told us plainly: "We spend hours reading PDFs to answer questions we should be able to ask in seconds. And when an auditor asks where a finding came from, we struggle to point to the exact source."

So we sat with them, mapped the workflow, and built a solution.

We delivered FinServe AI Copilot, an enterprise platform that turns their documents into instant, source-cited intelligence:

- Chat with any uploaded document and get answers with exact citations
- Score portfolio risk with explainable drivers and mitigation steps
- Run automated AML, KYC, GDPR and SOC 2 compliance reviews with coverage scoring
- Track every analysis in one executive dashboard

Under the hood it is production-grade, not a prototype. FastAPI backend, JWT authentication, vector-based retrieval, dual-mode AI (OpenAI or an offline engine), database persistence, and a clean Bootstrap dashboard, all Dockerized.

We shipped the MVP, the team validated it on their real data, and it worked.

Now we are in the post-MVP phase on an updated contract, adding more frameworks, deeper reporting, and tighter workflow automation. The client is happy, and so are we. That is how we like to work: start with the real problem, ship something that works, then grow it together.
